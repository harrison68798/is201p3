What is the Corrvett C6 Z06?

the Corrvett C6 Z06 is an American muscle car produced by Chevrolet, the daughter company of General motors, first produced in the year 2006 and ending its production in the 2013.

Powerplant

Powering the Corrvett C6 Z06 is a natrually asperated 7.0L LS7 pushrod V8 producing 505hp and 470lb-ft of torque making this Corrvett 0-60 in 3.5s.

Transmission

Putting down the power from the motor is a rear-mounted 6 speed manual transsmision called the Tremec Tr-6060. The C6 Z06 never came with an automatic transsmission.

Driving Experience

The driving experience of the Corrvett C6 Z06 is a force to be reckoned with thanks to the nearly perfect 49.3%/50.7% weight distribution making it a near perfect driving expereince.
